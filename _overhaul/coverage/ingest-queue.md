# Ingest Queue — Full-Page BIRAC Worklist (S5 tiering / Phase-B target)

> The **full-page set**: every confirmed-miss that earns a standalone BIRAC page under **Decision 1 = TIER**, plus the **5 borderline-ingests** from Decision 2. A miss earns a page if its holding is **currently good law** OR it is **foundational/doctrine-defining** (even if later refined) OR it is a **famous teaching FOIL**. Default-to-PAGE governs every uncertain call. Cases here are **pending** until Phase-B confirms CL identity + current treatment at ingest (a page may be demoted later; none is silently dropped).
>
> **Size: 171** = 166 page-earning confirmed-misses (of the 170 remaining after 15 authored) + 5 borderline-ingests (O'Connor v. Ortega, Quon, Hudson v. Palmer, Salinas v. Texas, Heck v. Humphrey).
>
> **Ordering = batch plan.** Grouped by home doctrine into ingest batches A–O. Tier-reason: `good-law` (holding governs now) · `foundational` (doctrine-defining anchor, even if refined) · `foil` (overruled/limited but a famous case instructors name to teach the modern rule).
>
> Columns: Case · Holding / ratio · Proposed home(s) + role · Diag-class · Tier-reason. Borderline-ingests flagged **[BL]**.
