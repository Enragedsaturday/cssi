# SPEC — Special Needs and Administrative Searches
slug: `special-needs-administrative-searches` · bucket: `exceptions` · status: NEW · template: Rule · Key cases (table: case · holding · weight · CL link) · Nuances & limits · Common pitfalls · Visual (Mermaid if multi-factor) · Flashcards (2-5 ::) · Sources

## Scope
Searches/seizures serving a 'special need' beyond ordinary law enforcement, judged by a reasonableness balancing rather than warrant+PC: sobriety checkpoints, school searches, probation/parole, government employees, regulated industries, drug-testing. Distinguish programmatic primary purpose (Edmond) from valid special needs.

  - conflating special-needs reasonableness with PC
  - forgetting it's a balancing test not a warrant exception in the usual sense
