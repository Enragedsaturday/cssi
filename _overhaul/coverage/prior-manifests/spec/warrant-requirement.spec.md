# SPEC — The Warrant Requirement
slug: `warrant-requirement` · bucket: `warrant` · status: NEW · template: Rule · Key cases (table: case · holding · weight · CL link) · Nuances & limits · Common pitfalls · Visual (Mermaid if multi-factor) · Flashcards (2-5 ::) · Sources

## Scope
What a valid warrant requires and how it fails: probable cause shown to a neutral and detached magistrate, supported by oath/affirmation, describing with particularity the place to be searched and things to be seized. Covers Franks challenges, knock-and-announce, anticipatory warrants, particularity/overbreadth, and staleness.

## Required cases by ROLE (status: MANIFEST=manifest-grade · DECK=NTC existence+holding+url · P1=verified Phase 1 · GAP=verify Phase 3)
  - **Johnson v. United States** — _ANCHOR — the inferences must be drawn by a neutral magistrate, not the officer engaged in the hunt_ · SCOTUS · `GAP`
  - **Illinois v. Gates** — _ANCHOR — totality basis for the magistrate's PC determination (cross-link PC/RS)_ · SCOTUS · `DECK`
  - **Franks v. Delaware** — _ANCHOR — a warrant may be voided if the affidavit contains a knowing/reckless material falsehood_ · SCOTUS · `DECK`
  - **Maryland v. Garrison** — _PROGENY — particularity & reasonable mistake (wrong apartment)_ · SCOTUS · `DECK`
  - **Groh v. Ramirez** — _PROGENY — a warrant that fails to describe the things to be seized is facially invalid_ · SCOTUS · `DECK`
  - **Andresen v. Maryland** — _PROGENY — business-records particularity; not a 5A violation_ · SCOTUS · `GAP`
  - **Lo-Ji Sales v. New York** — _LIMIT — open-ended/general warrant + non-neutral magistrate invalid_ · SCOTUS · `GAP`
  - **Wilson v. Arkansas** — _ANCHOR — knock-and-announce is part of 4A reasonableness_ · SCOTUS · `DECK`
  - **Richards v. Wisconsin** — _PROGENY — no blanket exception; no-knock needs RS of danger/destruction_ · SCOTUS · `DECK`
  - **Hudson v. Michigan** — _LIMIT — knock-and-announce violation does not trigger exclusion_ · SCOTUS · `DECK`
  - **United States v. Grubbs** — _PROGENY — anticipatory warrants are valid; triggering condition_ · SCOTUS · `DECK`

## Visual (Mermaid)
Valid warrant = PC + oath/affirmation + neutral & detached magistrate + particularity (place + things). Failure modes: Franks falsehood / general warrant (Lo-Ji) / no particularity (Groh).

## Common pitfalls (must cover)
  - officer acting as his own magistrate (Johnson)
  - general/overbroad descriptions
  - assuming knock-and-announce violations suppress evidence (Hudson)
